Why Should I Care About Service-Learning?

Service-Learning integrates life experiences with academia to promote educational success and personal growth by granting students the opportunity to connect with their local community and, potentially, beyond. By developing a connection with local communities, students are able to further their education by understanding the purpose and application of knowledge gained from the classroom to the real world.
